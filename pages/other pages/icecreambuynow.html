About :

A chocolate brownie or simply a brownie is a chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms

and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density. Brownies often, but not always, have a glossy "skin"

on their upper crust. They may also include nuts,frosting, cream cheese, chocolate chips, or other ingredients.

A variation made with brown sugar and vanilla rather than chocolate in the batter is called a blond brownie

or blondie.
