About the movie

Master is a 2021 Indian Tamil-language action drama film[3] written and directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj.

Produced by Xavier Britto,under his maiden production house XB Film Creators, the film stars Vijay and Vijay Sethupathi

with Malavika Mohanan, Shanthanu Bhagyaraj, Andrea Jeremiah, Arjun Das and Gouri G. Kishan play other supporting roles.

The film revolves around an alcoholic professor, J. D. (Vijay), who takes a three-month teaching job in a juvenile home and

clashes with a ruthless gangster named Bhavani (Vijay Sethupathi), who uses the children as the scapegoat for his criminal activities.
